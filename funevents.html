Handicraft Exhibit & Contest (9 Jan)

This competition is not like all the others. We value creativity, skill, timing, and interest. There are no bounds on what you make. Come with all the required material and give a shape to it in the stipulated time and the best crafts made and displayed in the given time will be rewarded with the exciting cash prize.

Team Size: 4 (maximum)

Prize Money:

  • 1st Prize - INR 1000/-
  • 2nd Prize - INR 500/-
  • 3rd Prize - INR 300/-

Rules and Guidelines:

  1. Craft should be built from scratch in the arena only.
  2. The teams that come with pre-built crafts will be disqualified.
  3. It is the responsibility of the teams to bring all the required material.
  4. Maximum of 2 hours will be allocated to all the participants for making and displaying the craft starting from 4:30 PM on the day of the competition.
  5. Any participant resorting to the inconvenience of others will be disqualified and will be asked to leave the arena.
  6. Any participant, if found violating the rules shall be disqualified.
  7. Damaging the property of the university will be considered a violation of rules.
  8. Photographs and videos, taken by volunteers during the competition may be used for records and publicity.
  9. The decision of judges will be final in the evaluation of crafts and no further arguments will be considered.
  10. Participants arguing with the judges will not be entertained.
  11. Judges possess all rights to alter any detail specified in this document and to amend necessary rules for conducting the event effectively.

Registration fee (Per Individual): INR 50/-

You can register for this event at the registration desk setup in the first floor of the college.
